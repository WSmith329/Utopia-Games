FAR CRY 6

Welcome to Yara, a tropical paradise frozen in time. Far Cry 6 immerses players into the adrenaline-filled world of a modern-day guerrilla revolution.

Join the revolution and push back against the oppressive regime of dictator Antón Castillo and his teenage son Diego, brought to life by Hollywood stars Giancarlo Esposito (The Mandalorian, Breaking Bad) and Anthony Gonzalez (Coco).

Playing as Dani Rojas, immerse yourself in the journey of a military dropout turned guerrilla revolutionary. To even the odds against Antón’s military, you’ll have to adopt the Resolver philosophy, employing an arsenal of unique and surprising new weapons, vehicles and animal companions to ignite a revolutionary movement that will burn the tyrannical regime to the ground.

Become a guerrilla

Play Dani Rojas as male or female and experience a compelling storyline that takes you from being a reluctant citizen to a leader fighting against Antón's regime. Jump into the action and chaos of guerrilla combat and gather soldiers and resources to help you in the fight.

All new animal companions

No guerrilla should go at it alone. Pair up with all-new animal companions like Chorizo, the adorable wiener dog no enemy soldier can ignore, and Guapo, the hungry pet crocodile of your guerrilla mentor, Juan Cortez.

Power of an army

Embrace the Resolver philosophy and create a deadly arsenal of makeshift weapons. Give Antón’s troops a taste of guerrilla ingenuity, from a motorcycle engine powered minigun to homing missile throwing backpacks.

Yara torn apart

Explore the largest Far Cry to date as you traverse jungles, beaches and urban landscapes. From taking the reins of a horse to commandeering a tank, choose your favourite ride to navigate an entire island nation.
