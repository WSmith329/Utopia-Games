About Us

Our Team

Our team truly is the core of our company. Everyone who works for Utopia Games is an essential cog in the beautiful machine that provides you with your favourite video games.

A young team, we have only been around since 2020 but have already made our splash in the scene. Based in Nottingham, we ship across the whole of the UK for the best prices.

Aside from video games, we provide game consoles, gaming accessories, various toys and have also recently launched our own merchandise line. As well as providing games, we dedicate pages of our website to upcoming games, newly released games and also our personal favourite games.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide everyone, no matter who they are or the extent of their gaming experience, with the best quality, hottest new releases and most anticipated upcoming video games in the most environmently-friendly way.

Our Values:
  • Loyalty to our compatriots.
  • Honesty with our investors.
  • Trust within our team.
  • Accountability for our operations.
  • Respect for any-and-everyone.
